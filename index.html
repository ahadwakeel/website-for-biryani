CHICKEN BIRYANI

Biryani is favourite Dish in Pakistan

INGRDIENTS

  • 1 kg chicken
  • 1 kg Banspati Rice
  • 00 gm onions sliced
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 4 medium sliced Tomatos
  • 6 Green chilies
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • Biryani Masala
  • 4 green Cardamom
  • 4 Cloves
  • 2 tbsp Salt
  • 1 cup Mint Leaves

METHOD

  1. Add Oil to a deep Pain on a low heat until hot.add in five chopped on medium size onion an fry to for 10 to 12 minutes until soft and light brown
  2. Add to the onions some ginger garlic paste. and stir and fry for two Minutes low heat Add the chicken (either on or the bone) and cook on high heatfor two to three Minutes
  3. Add the Biryani masala Powder, a splash of a water and stir. fry on Medium Heat for two to Three Minutes
  4. Add four Medim sliced Tomatos, stir and fry for four minutes on low heat until the tomatoes are soft
  5. Add the Yogurt and stir together. add the Coriander, Chopped mint,and cook on a low heat for two minutes
  6. Add the green chili, cover the the pain.and cook on a low heat for twenty minutes. stir occasnailly
  7. Add the cloves cinnamom, and cardamos to a pan of boilling water.add the caraway seeds, salt, and stir
  8. Add some miint leaves, and cook on a low heat for seven to eight minutes
  9. Mix the Rice chicken evenly to coat the rice with the chicken and suace
  10. Your Chicken Biryani is now ready to serve
